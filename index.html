Full Story Starts here: Once upon a time, in a world made entirely of pixels, a group of pixels became twisted and turned into mischievous little creatures. They loved nothing more than to play pranks on the other pixels, causing all sorts of chaos and confusion.
One day, the twisted pixels decided to play a trick on the biggest, strongest pixel in the land, who was known for his impressive muscles and fierce scowl. The twisted pixels snuck up behind him and tied his pixels into knots, leaving him completely tangled and helpless. The other pixels laughed and laughed at the sight of the big, tough pixel all tied up like a pretzel. And from that day on, the twisted pixels were known as the greatest pranksters in all the land.
